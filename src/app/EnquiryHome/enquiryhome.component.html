Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become an enquirer?

Simply click the 'Signup as Enquirer' button and fill out the registration form.

What are the benefits of becoming an enquirer?

In order to open a Account in our Bank you need to become an enquirer, and then you can fill the necessary information needed to open an account. Once all the necessary Information is filled a Manager from our Bank will be assigned to you which will communicate with you further.

Is there a fee to become an enquirer?

No, signing up as an enquirer is completely free.
